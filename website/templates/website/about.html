About This Project

Topiq — Smart Study
Resource Finder

An intelligent platform that helps university students find the best learning resources for any topic — instantly.

What is Topiq?

Topiq is a smart study resource recommendation system designed as a final-year project. It aggregates video lectures, articles, blogs, and reading materials into one unified search experience for university students — powered by an intelligent recommendation engine that ranks the most relevant content.

Why Was It Built?

University students waste significant time searching across YouTube, Google, and multiple websites to find quality study materials. Topiq solves this by providing a single, intelligent search point that ranks and recommends the most relevant resources — saving hours of manual filtering.

Real-World Impact

Problems Topiq Solves

01

Fragmented Resources

Learning materials are scattered across YouTube, blogs, PDFs, and university portals with no single access point.

02

Wasted Time

Students spend hours filtering through low-quality or irrelevant content before finding useful materials.

03

No Smart Ranking

Generic search engines don't provide personalized or topic-aware resource ranking for university syllabi.

04

No Study Assistant

Students lack an academic-focused AI assistant for quick clarifications without leaving their study flow.
