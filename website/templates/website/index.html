Can you explain the four necessary conditions for deadlock?
AI
Great question! The four necessary conditions for deadlock (known as the Coffman Conditions) are:

1. Mutual Exclusion – Resources can't be shared simultaneously
2. Hold & Wait – A process holds a resource while waiting for others
3. No Preemption – Resources can't be forcibly taken
4. Circular Wait – A circular chain of processes waiting on each other

All four must hold simultaneously for deadlock to occur. Would you like examples? 🎯
